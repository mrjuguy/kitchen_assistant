# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 27, 2026**

Kitchen Assistant ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: When you create an account, we may collect your name and email address.
- **Household Data**: We collect information about your household, including names of household members you invite.
- **Pantry & Shopping Data**: We store your inventory items, shopping lists, and custom product settings (like expiry preferences).
- **Recipes**: Any recipes you save or create within the app.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data**: We may collect information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and time spent.
- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your mobile device, including model and OS version.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain the service.
- To synchronize data between members of your household.
- To provide personalized recipe recommendations based on your pantry and preferences.
- To improve and optimize the app experience.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We use **Supabase** (a backend-as-a-service) to store your data securely. Your data is protected by industry-standard security measures provided by our infrastructure partners.

## 4. Sharing of Information
- **Household Members**: Your pantry and shopping list data is shared with other members of your designated household.
- **Third-Party Services**: We use OpenFoodFacts to provide product nutritional information. This service does not receive your personal data.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose information if required by law.

## 5. Your Choices & Data Deletion
- **Account Deletion**: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the Profile screen in the app.
- **Household Management**: You can leave a household or remove members at any time.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Kitchen Assistant is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@kitchenassistant.app.
